The beauty

During hiking you are captivated by the amazing beauty of nature, whether it be mountains, rivers or forests. You can, of course, say: "I saw your mountains on TV!" and limit yourself to viewing photos, but that's completely different.

Sports recreation

If, after a normal vacation, girls are puzzled by the question of how to lose weight now, few people succeed in gaining excess weight on campaigns. Losing is much more real.

Faithful friends

Friendship is not very easy on hikes - it’s hard for everyone and people don’t try to look better than they really are. But it has been developing for centuries - friendship, tested by difficulties, is very different from urban friendships.

Reboot

Office workers often complain that they do not have time to really take a break from work, because they continue to think about it during the holidays. I would advise such people to go camping. Verified - thoughts about the problems associated with work, school, relationships and other city life cease to worry you on the first day of the trip, and until the end of the route you don’t remember them. This is a real reboot of the head.

Bonfire and guitar.

Before I headed to the mountain club, the hikes seemed to me like this: evening, campfire gatherings, a bonfire and guitar songs. Reality has made its own corrections: it turns out that bonfires in mountain hikes are rarely burned, because there are few trees at a height, and food is cooked on gas burners. The guitar is also usually not taken - overweight. And, frankly, in the evening everyone who comes to the parking lot is so tired that there’s no time for songs. But at all training exits, tour gatherings and hiking weekend the guitar is present without fail.
